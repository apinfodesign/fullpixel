About FullPixel


The world is moving toward mobile, but many people are still using large screens with increasing pixel densities.


FullPixel was designed as a photosharing application to enable people to go big - sharing the beauty of their large images in a way that can be appreciated on the highest resolution screens commonly available in the market.


FullPixel is an Angular single page application, with a Mongo database.

Full Pixel was a learning project - all of us would do things differently now that we've learned from building this application!
