About the Full Pixel Project

The world is moving toward mobile, but many people are still choosing to purchase large screens with increasing pixel densities.

FullPixel was designed as a photosharing application to enable people to go big - sharing the beauty of their large images in a way that can be appreciated on the highest resolution screens in the market.

FullPixel is an Angular SPA (single page application) with a Mongo database. It includes user registration and login functionality.

Full Pixel was a learning project - all of us would do things differently now that we've learned from building this application!
